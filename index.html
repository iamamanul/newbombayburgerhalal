About Us

Welcome to New Bombay Burger Halal, your go-to destination for delicious and authentic fast food in the heart of New Delhi! Located at F-147, Nala Road, Jamia Nagar, Shaheen Bagh, our eatery is dedicated to serving the best of halal cuisine to our valued customers.

Our Story

At New Bombay Burger Halal, we're passionate about providing a unique dining experience that combines great taste, quality, and convenience. Our journey began with a simple idea - to serve mouth-watering fast food that's not only delicious but also halal, catering to the diverse tastes and preferences of our customers. Today, we're proud to be one of the most popular eateries in the city, loved by locals and visitors alike.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide exceptional fast food that's not only tasty but also hygienic and halal. We're committed to using only the freshest ingredients, sourced from trusted suppliers, to ensure that every bite is a delight for your taste buds. Our team of experienced chefs and friendly staff are dedicated to providing a warm and welcoming atmosphere, making you feel at home every time you visit us.
